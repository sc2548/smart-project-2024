About

Better Seed Better Yield

Since 2020, we have proudly held RICA licensing, guaranteeing quality-verified seeds.

We monitor disease and pest resistance to ensure optimal crop performance in our partners' fields.

Rooted in sustainability through smart crop rotation, we specialize in the following crops:

Maize

Maize stands as Rwanda's third-largest crop, predominantly cultivated by smallholder farmers (WFP,2022). This versatile crop is not only a staple in local diets but also serves as a key ingredient in various food products, contributing to food processing and animal feed industries.

High Iron Beans

Rwandans boast the world's highest per capita bean consumption. Beans provide households with a substantial 32% of the required calories and an impressive 65% of protein across the nation, underlining their indispensable contribution to the nutritional well-being of the population (RAB).

Soybeans

The cultivation of soybeans addresses food insecurities by offering a high protein content and contributes to mitigating the issues of poor livelihoods, elevated malnutrition levels, and food insecurity as highlighted by the RAB. Soybeans also enhance soil fertility in smallholder farms (RAB).
