Mission

Established in 2021, E&E Green Farms LTD is committed to strengthening farmersâ€™ capacity by providing high-quality seeds and training programs. Aligning with global agricultural goals, we recognize the vital roles women and youth can play in the agricultural sector. For this reason, we focus on fostering partnerships with women and youth. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to empower them, build a resilient farming community, and ensure a prosperous future with diversity and innovation.

Here are our approaches to sow the seeds of success:

TRAINING

We provide extension services and technical knowledge to improve farmers production and provide tools necessary to utlize appropriate technologies for higher yields.

PROCESSING

We carry out seeds processing activities including sorting, grading, packaging and branding to ensure farmers access seeds of high quality.

DISTRIBUTION

We partner with agro dealers, cooperatives and other company to ensure seeds reach farmers and ensuring a robust and sustainable agricultural system.

SEED PRODUCTION

We do seeds multiplication and work with our partners to monitor the seed production process to ensure farmers received seeds of high quality.
